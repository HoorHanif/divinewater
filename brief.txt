BRAND OVERVIEW
--------------
Brand Name    : Divine Water
Tagline       : Life in every drop.
Market        : Premium water brand
Style Target  : Ultra minimal, clean, $50,000 website feel
Reference     : VOSS Water Global


----------------------------------------------------------------
BRAND PERSONALITY
----------------------------------------------------------------
1. Pure     — clean, uncluttered, nothing unnecessary
2. Elevated — this is not a commodity, it is a premium product
3. Calm     — water is stillness, the site should breathe


----------------------------------------------------------------
COLOR PALETTE
----------------------------------------------------------------
Role                  Name              Hex
----                  ----              ---
Background            Pure White        #FAFAFA
Surface / Cards       Soft White        #FFFFFF
Primary Accent        Deep Navy         #0A1628
Secondary Accent      Ice Blue          #D6E8F5
Text Primary          Almost Black      #0D0D0D
Text Secondary        Cool Gray         #6B7280
Border                Pale Blue         #E5EEF5

Rationale:
White and navy is the language of luxury water brands globally.
It signals purity and premium quality without trying too hard.
Ice blue adds a subtle water reference without being obvious.


----------------------------------------------------------------
TYPOGRAPHY
----------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------
COPY TONE OF VOICE
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Short sentences. No filler words.
- Second person — "you" not "our customers"
- Think VOSS meets a luxury spa menu
- Every line should feel considered and deliberate
- No clichés. No "premium quality" or "best in class"

Example headline : "Water the way nature intended."
Example body     : "Divine is not filtered. It is not treated.
                   It is simply water — at its most honest."


----------------------------------------------------------------
DESIGN PRINCIPLES
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Generous white space — let the product breathe
- Typography does the heavy lifting, not graphics
- One strong color moment — the dark navy full-width section
- No stock photo clichés
- Product bottle is always the hero visual
- Smooth scroll, subtle animations, premium micro-interactions
- Mobile responsive — breakpoints at 480px, 768px, 1024px
- Built in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript


----------------------------------------------------------------
PAGE SECTIONS — 6 TOTAL
----------------------------------------------------------------

SECTION 1 — HERO
-----------------
Layout    : Full viewport, centered content
Background: Water video/animation provided by client
Content   : Brand logo (provided by client)
            Tagline — "Life in every drop." in Cormorant
            Garamond italic, large
            Subheading — "Premium water. Pure source.
            No compromises."
            CTA Button — "Discover Divine"
Feel      : Cinematic, still, breathtaking


SECTION 2 — ABOUT THE BRAND
-----------------------------
Layout    : Two column — text left, visual right
Content   : Short brand story paragraph
            "Divine Water is born from nature's most pristine
            sources, bottled with care and delivered to your
            table with nothing added and nothing removed."
            Minimal decorative element or product visual
Feel      : Editorial, calm, trustworthy


SECTION 3 — OUR PRODUCTS
--------------------------
Layout    : Three cards side by side
Content   : Product name, short description, size
            CTA on each card — "Order Now"
Examples  : Still Water / Sparkling Water / Premium Glass Bottle
Feel      : Clean white cards, subtle border, no heavy shadows


SECTION 4 — WHY DIVINE
------------------------
Layout    : 2x2 feature grid
Content   : 4 tiles — each with minimal icon, short title,
            one sentence description
Examples  :
  - 100% Natural Source
  - No Added Minerals
  - Eco-Conscious Packaging
  - Delivered Fresh
Feel      : Light, minimal, confident


SECTION 5 — THE SOURCE
------------------------
Layout    : Full width, dark navy background, white text
Content   : Editorial statement about the water's origin
            A pull quote or strong one-liner
Feel      : Premium contrast moment, cinematic, intentional


SECTION 6 — CONTACT & ORDER
-----------------------------
Layout    : Simple centered layout
Content   : Email — xyz@mail.com (placeholder, to be updated)
            Contact form — 3 fields: Name, Email, Message
            Social media links
Feel      : Clean, minimal, easy to use
